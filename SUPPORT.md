# Support

QRWarden is a development candidate and does not yet have a supported production release.

## Public questions and reports

Once public issue intake is enabled, use the repository's issue forms for reproducible bugs and focused feature requests. Search existing issues first and provide the exact commit or release version, browser, operating system, installation mode, expected behavior, and observed behavior.

Use synthetic QR payloads only. Never post credentials, personal data, private keys, production QR payloads, sensitive screenshots, or unredacted logs. Maintainers may close reports that cannot be investigated safely or that fall outside the documented product boundaries.

## Security and conduct

Report suspected vulnerabilities through the private process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Do not describe them in a public issue, discussion, or pull request.

Follow [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) for conduct concerns. Vulnerability advisories are not a conduct-reporting channel.

## Scope

The project does not provide emergency response, destination reputation checks, recovery of funds or accounts, private QR analysis, or assurances that a decoded destination is safe. QRWarden reports locally observable evidence and leaves the decision to the user.
